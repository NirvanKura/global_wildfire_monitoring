🔑 NASA FIRMS API Key Required Free

A free MAP_KEY from NASA FIRMS is needed to stream live satellite fire detections (VIIRS & MODIS) directly into this dashboard. No credit card required — takes under 60 seconds.

1
Visit firms.modaps.eosdis.nasa.gov/api/map_key — the official NASA Earthdata portal for FIRMS access.
2
Click "Sign Up" if you don't have an Earthdata account, or "Log In" if you do. University / institutional emails work best.
3
After logging in, click "Get MAP Key". Your key is generated instantly — copy the alphanumeric string (e.g., abc123xyz456).
4
Paste the key in the field below and hit Connect. The key is saved in your browser — you won't need to enter it again.
ℹ
Rate limit: 10 requests / minute per key. This dashboard auto-batches requests to stay within limits. Data refreshes every 5 minutes.
